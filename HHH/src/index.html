Our services

The services we provide is hair transplant, skin treatments, health related suggestions. With different facilities for men and women, we also provide skin treatments, reducing fat treatments, health, etc.

For Men

Transplantation of one’s own hair. Simply put, your own hair is moved to the place where your hair has fallen out or has become thinner and it is directed into the right growth direction.

For Women

Accentuation of the hairline, promotion of the density of hair, fill areas where the hair is receding from the temples possibly accentuate the ‘widow’s peak’ or the V-shaped hairline on the forehead.

For Facial and body hair treatment

Beard | Mustache | Chest. Using the same hair transplant techniques facial hair like sideburns, mustaches and beards can be created in any style.
